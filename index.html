Figuring out what climate change is and how it happens.




About Climate Change

Climate change is a serious issue that the Earth has been facing for many years. It refers to the change in usual weather patterns in a certain area. For instance, either rain, snow, or other types of precipitation could be imbalanced due to climate change. (Source)


The emission of fossil fuel by many factories is one of the contributing causes to climate change.


Since the rise in temperatures are melting ice caps, polar bears are being stripped from their natural, cold habitat. This poses a threat to their species.



What Can We Do?

The small changes that we can make in our lifestyles to limit the effects of climate change can be easy to make! This includes utilizing cleaner energy transportation (ex: cycling, carpooling) and turning off unused household electronics that uses coal, oil, and gas.
